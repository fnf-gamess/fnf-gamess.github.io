FNF Vs. Jason Voorhees: 13th Friday Night Funk

Featured FNF Mods

A Rhythm Battle Against a Horror Legend

FNF Vs. Jason Voorhees: 13th Friday Night Funk mixes rhythm gameplay with classic horror vibes. Inspired by the Friday the 13th movie series, this mod throws Boyfriend into a chilling encounter with Jason Voorhees, the masked killer known for his hockey mask and machete. The story begins during a camping trip. Boyfriend and Girlfriend join a group of campers for a quiet weekend in the woods. Unfortunately, curiosity leads them into a forbidden area near the infamous Crystal Lake territory. Their presence awakens Jason, who was resting in the darkness.

Songs You Will Face

This mod delivers a set of intense tracks with rock and metal influences, creating a dark atmosphere that perfectly fits the horror theme.

Tracklist in the mod:

  • Drowned Memories
  • Relentless
  • Blizzard
  • NK
  • Mother
  • Relentless OG

Version 1.5 introduced two additional songs:

  • Mother
  • NK (inspired by Julio Saenz’s Friday the 13th Animated video)

The song Relentless was also updated with improved graphics and a special gameplay mechanic that requires players to dodge certain attacks.

Gameplay Highlights

Rather than feeling like a typical FNF mod, this one adds a stronger sense of tension thanks to its horror theme.

Feature Description
Theme Horror / Slasher inspired
Opponent Jason Voorhees
Music Style Rock & Metal
Battles 6 rhythm battles
Special Mechanic Dodge system in Relentless

The dark visuals and intense sound design make every match feel like a fight for survival rather than just another rhythm duel.

How the Controls Work

Getting started is simple, but surviving the later songs requires concentration and fast reflexes.

Key Inputs

  • Arrow Keys – Match incoming notes
  • Enter – Start songs or confirm menu choices
  • Escape – Pause the game

When arrows move toward the target line, press the matching key at the exact moment they overlap. Accurate timing keeps Boyfriend’s performance meter on the winning side.

Helpful Tips

  • Watch patterns instead of reacting randomly.
  • Keep your focus on the beat of the music.
  • Stay calm during fast sequences—panic leads to missed notes.

With its horror setting, energetic soundtrack, and creative mechanics, FNF Vs. Jason Voorhees: 13th Friday Night Funk offers a thrilling twist on the classic Friday Night Funkin’ formula. It’s a great choice for players who enjoy rhythm games with a darker atmosphere.

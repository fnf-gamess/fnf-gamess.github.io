Email: contact@fnf-games.online

DMCA Email: dmca@fnf-games.online

General Inquiries and Support

If you encounter issues while playing Friday Night Funkin' or any FNF mod on our platform, please include the following information in your message to help us resolve your issue efficiently:

  • The URL of the game page
  • A description of the issue
  • Your browser type (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, etc.)
  • Device type (Desktop or Laptop)
  • Screenshots (if available)

Common issues may include:

  • Game not loading
  • Audio sync problems
  • Keyboard input delay
  • Display or scaling errors
  • Broken embedded content

Providing detailed technical information allows us to diagnose performance bottlenecks, JavaScript errors, or server-related latency faster.

Mod Developers & Content Creators

  • Want your FNF mod featured on our website

Please contact us at contact@fnf-games.online with:

  • Proof of ownership

We respect intellectual property and community guidelines. Our platform is structured to support creators while maintaining a positive ecosystem for players.

DMCA & Copyright Concerns

FNF-Games.online complies with applicable United States copyright laws and takes intellectual property rights seriously.

If you believe that any content hosted on our website infringes your copyright, please submit a formal DMCA notice to:

dmca@fnf-games.online

Your DMCA request should include:

  • Your full legal name and contact information
  • Identification of the copyrighted work
  • The exact URL of the allegedly infringing material
  • A statement of good faith belief
  • A statement under penalty of perjury confirming accuracy
  • Your physical or electronic signature

Upon receiving a valid DMCA complaint, we will review and take appropriate action, which may include removal or disabling access to the content.

Technical Reporting & Performance Feedback

Because rhythm games demand precision timing, performance is critical. If you experience:

  • Input lag
  • Audio desynchronization
  • Frame drops
  • Server latency

We encourage you to report these issues immediately. Our team continuously monitors performance metrics, particularly for users located in the United States, to ensure low-latency gameplay.

Detailed reports help us optimize: CDN distribution, asset compression, script execution efficiency, and mobile compatibility.

Security & Responsible Disclosure

If you discover a potential security vulnerability on FNF-Games.online, please report it responsibly via email. Include:

  • A description of the vulnerability
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Screenshots or proof of concept (if applicable)

We appreciate responsible disclosure and will investigate promptly.

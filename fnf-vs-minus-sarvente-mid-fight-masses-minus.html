Featured FNF Mods

What Is FNF vs Minus Sarvente (Mid-Fight Masses Minus)?

FNF vs Minus Sarvente (Mid-Fight Masses Minus) is a visual reimagining of the famous Mid-Fight Masses mod. Instead of introducing new characters or a different storyline, this version focuses on a Minus-style redesign that transforms Sarvente, Ruv, and other elements into bold, simplified versions of themselves.

The Minus style uses brighter colors, thick outlines, and minimalistic shapes. Because of this, character animations appear cleaner and easier to read during fast rhythm sections. The mod has also been updated with a V-Slice web port, allowing smoother gameplay directly in the browser without requiring a downloadable EXE file.

For players who enjoyed the original Mid-Fight Masses, this version offers the same intense rhythm battles while presenting them in a fresh artistic style.

Gameplay Features

Players will notice several elements that make FNF vs Minus Sarvente (Mid-Fight Masses Minus) stand out:

  • Completely redesigned Minus-style characters
  • Clean and colorful visual presentation
  • Familiar Mid-Fight Masses soundtrack
  • Smooth performance thanks to the V-Slice port
  • Playable directly in a browser without installation

Even though the mechanics stay faithful to the original FNF gameplay, the improved readability of the visuals makes long note sequences easier to follow.

Playing the Game

The objective is simple: keep up with the rhythm and outperform your opponent. Notes scroll toward the target line, and you must press the correct key at the exact moment they align.

Basic Controls

  • W / A / S / D or Arrow Keys – Hit the matching notes
  • Enter – Start a song or confirm menu options
  • Esc – Pause the current battle

Staying focused on the beat is the key to success. Missing too many notes will push the performance meter toward your opponent, which could cost you the battle.

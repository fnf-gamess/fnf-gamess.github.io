FNF Sweet Licorice

Featured FNF Mods

A Picnic That Turns Into a Musical Challenge

A relaxing afternoon by the lake quickly turns into a rhythm battle in FNF Sweet Licorice. Boyfriend and Girlfriend were simply enjoying a quiet picnic surrounded by nature when two mysterious visitors suddenly appeared. The newcomers claim the couple has taken their favorite spot, and instead of arguing normally, the disagreement turns into a musical showdown.

The scene feels lighthearted rather than hostile. Curious bystanders gather around while the two couples settle their dispute the only way characters in the rhythm world know how—through music. This setup gives the mod a playful story moment that feels different from typical stage-based encounters. Despite being a short experience, FNF Sweet Licorice still delivers memorable character interactions and an entertaining atmosphere.

Meet the Main Opponent

Matzu – The Cheerful Challenger

The primary singer you face during the battle is Matzu, who performs together with her girlfriend Melodia. Matzu stands out thanks to her unusual appearance and expressive personality.

Feature Matzu's Details
Form Cyan creature with a water-like body
Visuals Long flowing hair and a flower hairpin
Style Wears colorful accessories and a skirt
Personality Often appears cheerful and energetic

Interestingly, Matzu’s physical form is connected to the flower hairpin she wears. According to the character’s lore, her body is contained within that accessory. If it were removed, she would collapse into a puddle.

Another fun fact is that Matzu’s vocal sounds are sampled from the song “Antonymph” by Vylet Pony, giving her singing style a distinctive tone.

Song Style and Musical Inspiration

The soundtrack blends multiple musical influences into a single performance. Instead of relying purely on original vocals, the mod creatively mixes samples and references from different artists.

  • Vocal samples inspired by Vylet Pony
  • Melodic influence from Evanescence
  • Small lyrical references that fans of Deltarune might recognize

Because of this mix of influences, the song feels slightly experimental while still fitting the rhythm gameplay style players expect.

Gameplay Basics

Like most rhythm mods, the battle focuses on timing and accuracy. Players must follow the arrows that appear on the screen and press the matching keys when they align with the markers.

Key Action
Arrow Keys Hit the notes precisely
W A S D Alternate control layout
Enter Start or confirm menu selections
Esc Pause the song anytime

Maintaining good timing keeps your performance meter stable, while repeated misses may cause the battle to slip away.

Quick Facts Players Might Not Notice

Matzu’s animation sprites were reportedly completed in one day

The character design was inspired partly by fantasy cartoon creatures

The relationship between Matzu and Melodia was originally experimental

The characters are planned to appear again in a future project

Even though FNF Sweet Licorice is only a one-shot mod, its charming story setup and unique character design make the musical encounter memorable for players who enjoy creative rhythm battles.

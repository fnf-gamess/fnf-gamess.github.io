FNF Minus

Featured FNF Mods

A Fresh Visual Style for a Classic Rhythm Game

FNF Minus is a creative redesign of the original Friday Night Funkin’ experience. Instead of changing the core gameplay or adding a completely new story, this mod focuses on transforming how the characters look and feel. Every character from Week 1 to Week 6 receives a brand-new design inspired by the small icons that normally appear on the health bar during battles.

Familiar characters like Daddy Dearest, Pico, and Skid & Pump suddenly appear with bold shapes, exaggerated expressions, and colorful outlines that make them stand out from the original designs. What used to be simple facial icons are now expanded into full character styles, giving the entire game a unique “Minus” aesthetic.

Timing remains the most important skill. Listening closely to the rhythm will help maintain long combos and avoid missing notes while enjoying the reimagined visuals.

Development and Community Impact

The mod was created by animator IagoAnims with contributions from several designers and programmers. Thanks to its distinctive style, FNF Minus quickly became one of the most recognizable visual mods in the Friday Night Funkin’ community. Many players appreciated how it refreshed the original weeks without changing the core gameplay.

"Even though development eventually stopped, the mod remains widely remembered because of its bold art direction and creative reinterpretation of classic characters."

FNF Kick Kick Funk Vs. Dan The Man

Featured FNF Mods

Quick Overview of the Mod

Element Information
Mod Title FNF Kick Kick Funk Vs. Dan The Man
Game Type Rhythm / Music Battle
Main Opponent Dan
Featured Characters Boyfriend, Pico
Additional Appearances Girlfriend, Nene, Josie
Total Songs 2

FNF Kick Kick Funk Vs. Dan The Man is a crossover mod that connects the rhythm world of Friday Night Funkin’ with the action universe of Dan The Man. Instead of a typical rap battle, the mod delivers a performance that feels closer to a martial arts showdown where every beat matches the energy of a punch or kick.

Music Battles in the Mod

Players will face Dan through two original tracks designed specifically for this crossover. Each song captures the energetic personality of the characters and the fast-paced spirit of the Dan The Man series.

Song lineup

  • Funky Slam – A high-energy opening duel where Dan challenges Boyfriend.
  • Ginger – A faster and more intense follow-up battle featuring Pico joining the performance.

These tracks combine rhythm gameplay with a fighting-game atmosphere, making every note feel like part of an action sequence.

What Makes This Mod Unique?

Rather than adding complex mechanics, the mod focuses on style and presentation. The goal is to recreate the feeling of an arcade fight while keeping the classic FNF gameplay intact.

Key aspects players will notice:

  • Smooth animations inspired by the Dan The Man beat ’em up style
  • Expressive character movements during musical duels
  • Background spectators cheering during performances
  • Fast-paced tracks designed for energetic rhythm play

This approach keeps the gameplay easy to understand while still delivering a lively crossover experience.

Gameplay Basics

Like most Friday Night Funkin’ mods, the objective is to follow the rhythm and maintain your performance bar throughout the song. During a battle you should:

  • Watch the directional arrows moving toward the hit markers.
  • Press the matching direction when the arrows line up.
  • Keep a consistent combo to avoid losing the performance meter.

If you stay in sync with the music until the end of the track, you will defeat Dan in the rhythm duel.

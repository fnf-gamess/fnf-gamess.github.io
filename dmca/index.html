DMCA Policy – FNF-Games.online

Effective Date: March 3, 2026

FNF-Games.online respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects users, contributors, and partners to do the same. This Digital Millennium Copyright Act (“DMCA”) Policy explains how we address copyright infringement claims in accordance with the United States Digital Millennium Copyright Act (17 U.S.C. § 512).

If you believe that material available on FNF-Games.online infringes upon your copyrighted work, please review the procedures outlined below.

1. Overview of Our Platform

FNF-Games.online provides browser-based access to rhythm games and related content, primarily centered around Friday Night Funkin' and community-created modifications (“mods”).

We do not claim ownership of third-party intellectual property unless explicitly stated. Content displayed on the website may include:

  • Embedded game files
  • Community-developed FNF mods
  • Visual assets associated with gameplay
  • Descriptive or editorial content

Our goal is to provide access while respecting creator rights and complying with U.S. copyright law.

2. Filing a DMCA Takedown Notice

If you are a copyright owner, or authorized to act on behalf of one, and believe that material hosted on FNF-Games.online infringes your copyright, you must submit a written DMCA notice including all of the following elements:

  • Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed.
  • Identification of the infringing material, including the exact URL(s) where the material appears.
  • Your full legal name, mailing address, telephone number, and email address.
  • A statement that you have a good faith belief that the disputed use is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
  • A statement that the information in your notice is accurate and, under penalty of perjury, that you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner.
  • Your physical or electronic signature.

Incomplete or inaccurate DMCA notices may delay processing.

3. Submission of DMCA Notices

All DMCA notices must be submitted via email to:

DMCA Contact Email: dmca@fnf-games.online

Please use the subject line: “DMCA Takedown Request – [Your Name or Company]”

We recommend providing documentation or proof of ownership to expedite review.

4. What Happens After a DMCA Notice Is Received?

Upon receiving a valid DMCA notice:

  • We will review the claim promptly.
  • The allegedly infringing material may be removed or disabled.
  • The party responsible for the content may be notified.
  • A record of the claim will be maintained for legal compliance.

We aim to process legitimate requests within a reasonable timeframe, typically within 2–5 business days.

FNF-Games.online reserves the right to remove content without prior notice if a claim appears credible.

5. Counter-Notification Procedure

If you believe that content removed due to a DMCA notice was taken down in error or misidentification, you may submit a counter-notification.

Your counter-notification must include:

  • Identification of the material that was removed and its previous location (URL).
  • Your full contact information.
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief the material was removed due to mistake or misidentification.
  • A statement consenting to jurisdiction in a U.S. federal district court.
  • Your physical or electronic signature.

Counter-notifications must be sent to: dmca@fnf-games.online

If we receive a valid counter-notice, we may restore the content unless the original complainant files a court action within the legally required timeframe.

6. Repeat Infringer Policy

FNF-Games.online maintains a policy of terminating access to content associated with repeat infringers where appropriate and consistent with applicable law.

If we determine that a contributor repeatedly violates copyright rights:

  • Their content may be permanently removed.
  • Future submissions may be restricted.
  • Access privileges may be revoked.

We evaluate repeat infringement claims carefully and in accordance with the DMCA safe harbor framework.

7. Good Faith and Abuse of DMCA Process

Submitting false DMCA claims may result in legal liability under U.S. law. Similarly, filing fraudulent counter-notifications may carry legal consequences.

We encourage copyright holders and claimants to ensure accuracy before submitting notices.

FNF-Games.online acts as an online service provider under 17 U.S.C. § 512 and seeks to maintain eligibility for DMCA safe harbor protections.

8. Third-Party Content Disclaimer

FNF-Games.online may display or embed third-party game content for accessibility and convenience. We do not guarantee ownership or licensing status of all external content.

We respond to copyright concerns in good faith and strive to cooperate with rights holders.

9. Policy Updates

This DMCA Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in legal requirements or operational practices. Users are encouraged to review this page regularly for updates.

Continued use of the website after modifications to this policy constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

FNF-Games.online is committed to balancing accessibility to browser-based rhythm games with respect for intellectual property rights. We support creators and rights holders and aim to maintain a legally compliant and transparent platform for players across the United States.

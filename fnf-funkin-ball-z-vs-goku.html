FNF Funkin Ball Z vs Goku

Featured FNF Mods

A Dragon Ball Inspired Rhythm Battle

FNF Funkin Ball Z vs Goku is a crossover rhythm mod that blends the energetic world of Friday Night Funkin’ with the legendary Dragon Ball universe. In this mod, Boyfriend finds himself stepping into a tournament arena where the challenge waiting for him is none other than Goku, the iconic Saiyan hero known by generations of anime fans.

Instead of fighting with punches or powerful energy attacks, the two competitors decide to settle the confrontation with music. The stage feels similar to a classic martial arts tournament from Dragon Ball, but the real battle happens through rhythm and timing.

Players must follow the beat and hit the correct notes while Goku gradually becomes more serious during the performance. The idea of facing such a famous character through rap battles gives the mod a playful crossover feeling that many fans enjoy.

Songs in the Mod

The musical challenge is divided into three different tracks, each increasing the intensity of the battle.

Song What to Expect
Soaring A warm-up rhythm duel test
Crimson Sun Faster beats and demanding patterns
Power Pole The final energetic performance

Gameplay Basics

stay in sync with the music. Press the correct keys when the notes align with the judgment line.

Controls:
  • Arrow Keys / WASD Hit notes
  • Enter Start / Pause
  • Space Confirm

As the songs progress, the note patterns become faster and more complex. Stay calm and follow the rhythm!

Visual Style & Atmosphere

The mod captures the spirit of Dragon Ball with anime-inspired designs and backgrounds resembling iconic locations. Dramatic poses and glowing effects enhance the intensity of the showdown.

Behind the Mod

Created by a collaborative team of artists, programmers, and musicians. Even though the project ended early, it delivers a memorable experience for anime and rhythm fans alike.

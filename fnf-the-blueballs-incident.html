FNF: The Blueballs Incident

Featured FNF Mods

A Dark and Unusual Mission in the Rhythm World

FNF: The Blueballs Incident is a unique and challenging mod of Friday Night Funkin’ that mixes rhythm gameplay with a strange and slightly unsettling storyline. Instead of a typical music battle for fun, this adventure places Boyfriend in the role of a special agent tasked with stopping a mysterious threat.

In this story, Boyfriend becomes Special Agent BlueBalls, working under the orders of Derpina, president of the Troll Containment Foundation. His mission: stop the dangerous Trollge, a creature spreading chaos across the city, using nothing but a powerful mission-grade microphone.

A Battle Against Trollge

The central encounter revolves around Trollge, a character known for his eerie appearance and emotional turmoil. Beneath the terrifying exterior, Trollge hides deep sadness; Boyfriend attempts not only to defeat him but to calm the destructive energy.

Bonus Tracks Included:

  • Trolling (fan-made Disorder mod)

Mod Creator

FNF: The Blueballs Incident was developed by MaliciousBunny, who designed the concept, story, and intensive gameplay experience. The mod is celebrated for its unusual narrative and the intense challenge it offers to the FNF community.

FNF Gumballs

Featured FNF Mods

A Retro-Style Rhythm Adventure

FNF Gumballs is a creative rhythm mod that grew far beyond its original idea. What began as a simple reskin of the remix track “Paco” eventually expanded into a complete mod with its own story, characters, and soundtrack. Because of this evolution, this mod feels more like a mini rhythm adventure rather than just a single-song remix. The visual style of this project is heavily inspired by classic PC games from the early 2000s, giving it a unique nostalgic feel that sets it apart from more modern mods.

Key Highlights of the Mod

Here are some elements that make this experience stand out among many other rhythm mods:

  • A total of 8 rap battles across different stages
  • Multiple well-known characters appear as opponents
  • Short cutscenes that connect the battles together
  • A retro aesthetic inspired by early PC games
  • Music created by several community composers

Because of this variety, FNF Gumballs constantly introduces something new as players progress through the songs.

Opponents You May Encounter

Throughout this mod, Boyfriend faces several unexpected challengers. Instead of focusing on one main rival, the mod brings a rotating cast of characters.

Some notable appearances

Character Role in the Battle

These encounters are often introduced with humorous dialogue, making the transitions between songs feel more like story moments.

Gameplay Basics

The core mechanics of this title follow the traditional rhythm format used in most Friday Night Funkin’ mods.

Basic Controls

Arrow Keys Hit the incoming notes
W A S D Alternative input layout
Enter Start or confirm options
Esc Pause the game

Simple Strategy

  • Focus on the song rhythm instead of reacting too quickly
  • Try to maintain long combo streaks
  • Practice difficult songs several times to learn the patterns

Quick FAQ

Is this a long mod?

Yes. Compared to many small mods, it offers multiple songs and story segments, giving players a longer experience.

Why does the mod look different?

The developers intentionally designed FNF Gumballs with a retro style inspired by early 2000s PC games.

Can beginners play it?

Yes. While some songs become challenging, most players can learn the rhythm patterns after a few attempts.

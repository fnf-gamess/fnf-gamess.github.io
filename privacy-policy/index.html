Privacy Policy – FNF-Games.online

Effective Date: March 3, 2026

Welcome to FNF-Games.online. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit https://fnf-games.online/ and use our browser-based gaming services.

FNF-Games.online primarily serves users located in the United States and is focused on providing access to online rhythm games, particularly Friday Night Funkin' and related community-created modifications.

By accessing or using our website, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information in the following categories:

A. Non-Personal Information (Automatically Collected)

When you access our website, certain technical data may be collected automatically, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • Referring URL
  • Pages visited
  • Date and time of access
  • Anonymous usage analytics

This data helps us improve website performance, optimize load speeds for U.S. users, and enhance gameplay stability.

B. Voluntarily Provided Information

We may collect personal information if you:

  • Contact us via email
  • Submit a DMCA notice
  • Report a technical issue
  • Request content removal

Such information may include: Full name, email address, contact details, and information contained in your message.

Note: We do not require account registration to play games on FNF-Games.online.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate and maintain our website
  • Improve loading speed and browser compatibility
  • Respond to inquiries and DMCA notices
  • Monitor and prevent technical abuse or fraud
  • Analyze usage trends for performance optimization
  • Ensure compliance with legal obligations

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

FNF-Games.online may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Improve website functionality
  • Store browser preferences
  • Analyze anonymous traffic patterns
  • Enhancement user experience

Cookies may include: Session cookies, Analytics cookies, and Performance monitoring cookies. Users can disable cookies in their browser settings; however, certain features may not function optimally.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Website analytics
  • Content delivery networks (CDNs)
  • Advertising partners (if applicable)
  • Embedded game hosting

These third parties may collect anonymous technical data according to their own privacy policies. FNF-Games.online does not control third-party data practices and encourages users to review external privacy policies.

5. Data Retention

We retain collected information only as long as necessary to fulfill legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce our policies, and improve platform stability. Emails and DMCA-related correspondence may be retained for compliance and record-keeping purposes.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect information from unauthorized access, data breaches, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of online transmission is 100% secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

FNF-Games.online is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 in compliance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately at: contact@fnf-games.online

8. California Privacy Rights (CCPA Notice)

If you are a California resident, you may have rights under the CCPA, including:

  • The right to request access to personal data collected
  • The right to request deletion of personal data
  • The right to know what categories of information are collected
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

To submit a CCPA request, please email: contact@fnf-games.online

9. International Users

Although FNF-Games.online primarily serves users in the United States, visitors from other jurisdictions may access the site. By using the website, you understand that your information may be processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Effective Date.” Continued use of the website after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

FNF-Games.online is committed to transparency, responsible data handling, and maintaining a secure browsing environment for rhythm game enthusiasts across the United States.
